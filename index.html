Service · Dine-In

Dine-In Sync — Zero Wait at the Table

The moment you place your order on ZeroWait, our GPS engine begins tracking your live location. As you travel to the restaurant, our smart algorithm calculates your real-time ETA accounting for live traffic conditions.

When you are exactly 10 minutes away, the kitchen receives an automatic "FIRE" alert — telling the chef to start cooking your meal right now. By the time you walk through the door, sit down, and settle in, your food arrives at your table within 60 seconds.

  • Live GPS tracks your ETA in real time
  • Kitchen fires at precisely the right moment
  • Food served within 60 seconds of arrival
  • Saves 20–30 minutes per meal visit
  • Works for solo dining and group orders

Service · Ride + Food

Ride + Food Sync — Book Both, Arrive Together

ZeroWait's most innovative feature: book a ride and order food simultaneously inside the same app. The rider's live GPS becomes the kitchen's trigger. As your ride approaches the restaurant, the kitchen fires at the exact right moment.

Your rider arrives at the restaurant — food is packed and ready. Zero waiting at the pickup. Rider picks it up and delivers to you without any delay.

  • One app for ride booking and food ordering
  • Rider GPS syncs the kitchen automatically
  • Rider never waits — food is always ready
  • No cold food from sitting too long
  • Faster delivery from restaurant to you
Feature · Restaurant

Smart Kitchen Dashboard — Your Chef's Co-Pilot

A digital tablet display replaces paper tickets in the kitchen. Every order flows through four clear stages: PAID HOLD → KITCHEN PREP → FIRE → SERVED. The system automatically moves orders through these stages based on the customer's live GPS.

Chefs no longer guess when to start cooking. The dashboard tells them exactly when to begin. Kitchen chaos during rush hour becomes an organized, efficient operation.

  • Runs on any existing tablet — no hardware cost
  • Automatic stage transitions based on GPS
  • Loud audio alert when kitchen should fire
  • Handles concurrent orders with capacity control
  • Business analytics: peak hours, popular dishes
Feature · Real-World Safety Net

"I'm Running Late" — One Tap Saves Your Meal

GPS isn't always perfect. Traffic jams, gas stops, last-minute detours — life happens. That's why ZeroWait has the "I'm Running Late" button prominently placed in every active order screen.

One tap sends an instant HOLD FIRE signal to the kitchen. The chef pauses preparation or keeps your dish in a warming stage. Your food stays fresh and hot for when you actually arrive — not cold from sitting on the pass for 20 minutes.

  • One tap — instant kitchen pause signal
  • Auto-triggers if GPS shows no movement for 5 min
  • Kitchen resumes when you start moving again
  • Food is never cold or overcooked on arrival
Feature · AI Intelligence

AI One-Tap Reorder — Your Lunch, Planned for You

ZeroWait learns your habits over time. If you order Butter Chicken every Tuesday at 1 PM, it will prompt you on Tuesday at 12:45 PM: "Want your usual?" — one tap and it's ordered and on its way to the kitchen.

With calendar sync, ZeroWait reads your schedule. Meeting ends at 12:30 PM? It nudges you at 12:15 to place your order so food is ready the moment you walk out of the conference room.

  • Learns your order patterns automatically
  • "Want your usual?" smart prompt at the right time
  • Calendar sync — orders around your meetings
  • One tap to confirm — zero scrolling or searching

Calendar Sync — ZeroWait Knows Your Day

Connect your Google or Outlook calendar and ZeroWait becomes your intelligent lunch planner. It reads when your meetings end, when your free slots are, and suggests the perfect time to order so your meal is timed to your day — not the other way around.

  • Google & Outlook calendar integration
  • Smart lunch prompts between meetings
  • "Your meeting ends at 12:30 — order now?"

Feature · Group Ordering

Group Ordering — Everyone Orders, Everyone Pays

The most requested feature for office teams. One person starts a Group Lobby and shares a link with their colleagues. Each person opens the link, adds their own food from their own phone, and pays individually — no more one person handling everyone's money.

The app tracks the group leader's GPS for ETA. Everyone's food fires at the same time. The whole group arrives and eats together — instantly.

  • Share a link — anyone can join the lobby
  • Each person orders and pays separately
  • One table, one ETA for the whole group
  • All food fires simultaneously — eat together
Feature · Table Selection

Visual Table Selection — Pick Your Exact Seat

The biggest anxiety with pre-ordering is: "What if I arrive and there are no seats?" ZeroWait solves this completely. Before placing your order, you see a live floor plan of the restaurant — just like picking a seat at a cinema.

Green tables are free. Yellow ones are currently eating and leaving soon. Red ones are occupied. Pick your exact table, and it's reserved for you before you even leave your desk.

  • Live restaurant floor plan — updates in real time
  • Green / yellow / red availability system
  • Pick window, booth, or specific table
  • Your seat is guaranteed before you arrive
